In Frontend Web class, I learnt to use a new language called HTML ( Hyper text Markup Language).

The trainer also influenced me to self-train and practice

In Frontend Mobile class, I learnt a new programming language mostly used in apps called Kotlin. The trained helped me understand:

  • Basic git commands used in linux terminal.

In Ux Research class, I learnt a lot about different companies and how they make their user experience research to benefit their market. The trainer helped me understand more about:

  • Types of research and how to make use of them
  • Different research methods used by companies like Twiga foods and Spotify

  • Case studies made by different companies on Ux research and how they helped them grow

In Ui/UX Design class, I learnt about how to use different shapes to come up with a nice and presentable design. The trainer helped me understand more about:

In Quality Assurance class, I learnt a lot about:

  • What a QA does in a company.

In Product Management, The trainer helped me learn about:

  • Different personas in the product management step of a tech company

In Data structrure class , The trainer helped me understand more about:
